Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Amoebix ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information within the Amoebix Social Automation System. This system is an internal tool designed strictly for authorized employees to manage our official brand social media accounts.

2. Data Collection via Third-Party APIs (TikTok & Meta)

To provide automated publishing features, our system integrates with the TikTok Developer API and Meta Graph API. During authorization, we collect the following minimum necessary data:

  • Basic profile information (Username, Profile ID) of our official brand accounts.
  • Authentication tokens (Access Tokens) required to publish content programmatically.

3. Data Usage

The data collected is used exclusively for internal operational purposes. Specifically, the tokens are used to execute scheduled API requests to publish sports, educational, and family-oriented graphics to Amoebix's own authorized social media channels. We do not use this data for targeted advertising, nor do we sell, rent, or share this data with any external third parties.

4. Data Deletion and Revocation Instructions (Important)

We respect the right to data deletion. If you are an authorized administrator and wish to revoke the system's access to a connected TikTok or Meta account, or if you request the complete deletion of stored authentication data from our local secure databases, you must contact our technical team immediately.

Data Deletion Request Contact:
Please email us at: contact@amoebix.net
We will process and confirm the deletion of all associated API tokens and account data within 3 business days.

5. Compliance with TikTok Terms

This application operates in strict compliance with the TikTok Developer Terms of Service and API policies. Access tokens are stored securely and never exposed to unauthorized personnel.
