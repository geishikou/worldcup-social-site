Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2026

1. General Overview

This Privacy Policy governs the data processing practices of the internal social media automation application operated by OVO芸術科学株式会社 (OVO Arts & Science Co., Ltd.). This system is restricted exclusively to authorized staff members for publishing promotional and educational content for our art school brand, “童画こども美術教室”.

2. Data Collection via Public APIs

To enable automated publishing pipelines, our system interfaces with third-party platforms, including the TikTok Developer API and Meta Graph API. Through authorization protocols, we retrieve and store the following programmatic data:

  • Public profile metadata (Username, Account ID) linked to our official brand handle practitioner_art.
  • OAuth secure access tokens necessary to broadcast graphical images and updates without manual intervention.

3. Express Purpose of Data Use

All data and access credentials obtained are used strictly for internal corporate communication and marketing purposes. The credentials allow our system to schedule and publish art student spotlights, course highlights, and school notifications onto our official accounts. We maintain a strict zero-sharing policy: no data is ever sold, transferred, or exposed to commercial third parties.

4. Token Revocation & Data Deletion Compliance

OVO Arts & Science fully complies with international data privacy laws and platform specific developer regulations regarding data erasure.
