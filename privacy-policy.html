Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

1. General Overview

This Privacy Policy governs the data processing practices of the Worldcup Social platform operated by OVO芸術科学株式会社 (OVO Arts & Science Co., Ltd.). This software ecosystem serves our creative networks and art school properties including “童画こども美術教室”, enabling parents, students, and educators to safely track lessons, review artwork progress, and broadcast achievements.

2. API Data Integration (TikTok & Meta)

To provide advanced creative sharing tools, Worldcup Social interfaces via verified public APIs with third-party social networks. Upon intentional connection by an authorized account holder, we store and handle the following information:

  • Public profile metadata (Username, Account ID) linked to our verified brand handles.
  • System access tokens necessary to programmatically publish selected artwork collections, lesson summaries, and sports project highlights directly to the connected feed.

3. Direct Usage and Purpose

Data retrieved through Worldcup Social is utilized solely to power our integrated multi-platform sharing features. This tool allows schools and instructors to share standout creative creations, lesson recaps, and visual media with parents and the public. OVO Arts & Science strictly prohibits selling or sharing this data with secondary third parties.
