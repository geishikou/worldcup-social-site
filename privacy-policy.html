Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

1. General Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how OVO芸術科学株式会社 (OVO Arts & Science Co., Ltd.) collects, uses, and safeguards information within the OVO Art Portal application. This software ecosystem serves our art school network, including “童画こども美術教室”, enabling registered parents, students, and educators to interact, manage course schedules, review lesson progress, and broadcast student accomplishments.

2. API Data Integration (TikTok & Meta)

To provide advanced creative showcasing tools, our system interfaces via verified public APIs with third-party social applications like TikTok and Meta. Upon intentional connection by an administrator or account holder, we store and handle the following information:

  • Public profile metadata (Username, Account Profile Image) of the connected brand handle practitioner_art.
  • System access tokens necessary to programmatically publish selected artwork collections, lesson summaries, and school project highlights directly to the account feed.

3. Direct Usage and Purpose

Data retrieved through developer integration is utilized solely to power our Integrated Multi-platform Showcasing service. This tool allows schools and instructors to share standout creative creations, lesson recaps, and visual media with parents and the broader public on social channels. OVO Arts & Science strictly prohibits the sharing, marketing, or selling of this data to secondary third parties or external data warehouses.

4. Your Data Control and Erasure Options

We believe in user transparency. Users can unbind their social media presence or request complete data removal from our secure application databases at any given time.
